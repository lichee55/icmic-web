GENERAL INFORMATION

The International Conference on Maritime IT Convergence (ICMIC) will be inaugurated in 2022 with aims to promote convergence activities of maritime and terrestrial communications as well as related wireless communications. Recognizing that maritime communications will usually involve ship-to-ship and ship-to-shore communication, maritime ICT technologies will bring some mature but still evolving terrestrial wired/wireless communication technologies to its own use for future smart maritime communications. More specifically, the conference will focus on addressing challenges of maritime communications with ICT convergence or advanced wireless communications over various industrial sectors, academia and practice engineers. The conference will include keynote speech sessions, invited special sessions and technical paper sessions.

SUBMISSIONS

ICMIC invites the submissions of original, unpublished research works that are currently not under review.
  • Full paper: 4 pages
  • Recent results: 1 page
Detailed formatting and submission guidelines are available at the conference website at http://icmic.org.

PRESENTATION

The accepted paper will be presented in an oral session. All accepted papers will appear in the ICMIC 2022 proceedings only if at least one of the authors register the conference to present the paper.

JOURNAL PUBLICATION

Extended versions of selected papers will be invited for publication in SCIE indexed journals such as ICT Express, Electronics, International Journal of Sensors, Wireless Communications and Control and other journals.
